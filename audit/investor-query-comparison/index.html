Predicate Search vs Rudy Tactical Similarity — Investor Demo

Type or pick a tactical query. We retrieve in two complementary modes: explicit football predicates (lane, zone, pressure, progression) over 7,306 SkillCorner windows, AND expert-label similarity over the same corpus using Rudy's tactical labels and learned embeddings. The two panels often agree; sometimes only one finds the moment. That contrast is the product.

Curated subset: MINER curation not yet available; all 16 queries from query_intents.json are rendered.

Internal investor demo — candidate retrieval, not exhaustive production search.
